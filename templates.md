# Legal Templates

Standardized templates for legal filings, subpoenas, and court dockets.

## Civil Suit

**Instructions:**
- Provide proof of service via text message until paper service is available.
- Text message must show the defendant's phone number at the top (DO NOT use their name).
- Service message must include: "Plaintiff v. Defendant" and a reference to the docket (e.g., "Find more info on the docket").
- Post the screenshot in the docket with the date of service.
- You have one week from posting on the docket to deliver service.

```text
Date of Filing:
Date of Incident:
Time of Incident: (AU/EU/NA)
Plaintiff's Name:
Plaintiff's Phone Number:
Plaintiff's Attorney:
Attorney's Phone Number:
Defendant's Name:
Defendant's Phone Number:

Statement of Claim:
(Three or four sentences as to what this claim is. You do not need to spell the whole thing out here.)

Substantiating Evidence:
(What is the evidence that substantiates this claim? You do NOT need to list ALL your evidence here. This is just to show your claim has merit.)

Relief Sought:
(Please list out each part of your requested relief, and then give the total amount.)
```
