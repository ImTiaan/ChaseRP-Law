# Penal Code

## Flying in Restricted Airspace

- **Code:** AC550  
- **Type:** MIS  
- **Description:** Flies over restricted airspace without authorization (e.g., Boilingbroke State Penn., Fort Zancudo, military aircraft carrier).  
- **Fine:** $1250  
- **Sentence:** 10 months  

---

## Negligent Operation of an Aircraft

- **Code:** AC551  
- **Type:** INF  
- **Description:** Operates an aircraft in a way that is considered negligent. Comes with a free review of your pilot's license by licensing officials!  
- **Fine:** $500  
- **Sentence:** None  

---

## Reckless Operation of an Aircraft

- **Code:** AC552  
- **Type:** MIS  
- **Description:** Operates an aircraft in a way that is considered reckless. Temporarily suspends Pilot's License until licensing review.  
- **Fine:** $2500  
- **Sentence:** 10 months  

---

## Possession of a Controlled Dangerous Substance

- **Code:** CC100  
- **Type:** MIS  
- **Description:** Possesses 4+ joints of legally distributed marijuana, or any amount of other marijuana products. Possesses prescription medication or other controlled substances without a prescription. Licenses may be provided for marijuana and limits proscribed by law.  
- **Fine:** $250  
- **Sentence:** 5 months  

---

## Felony Possession of a Controlled Dangerous Substance

- **Code:** CC102  
- **Type:** FEL  
- **Description:** Possesses 10+ joints of marijuana, 5+ marijuana seeds, 2+ marijuana plants, or 30g+ of other marijuana products. Possesses 10+ grams of Methamphetamine or 10+ grams of Cocaine. Possesses 7+ doses of prescription medication or other controlled substances without a prescription. Licenses may be provided for marijuana and limits proscribed by law.  
- **Fine:** $1000  
- **Sentence:** 30 months  

---

## Drug Trafficking

- **Code:** CC103  
- **Type:** FEL  
- **Special:** HUT  
- **Description:** Transports, distributes, or cultivates large quantities of illegal substances. For this crime, large quantities of marijuana are considered 40+ joints marijuana, 20+ marijuana seeds, 5+ marijuana plants, or 200g+ of marijuana product. Other large amounts are considered 200g+ methamphetamine, 100g+ of Cocaine, or 19+ doses of other prescriptions or controlled substances. Licenses may be provided for marijuana and limits proscribed by law. This is a Hold Until Trial Charge!  
- **Fine:** $0  
- **Sentence:** None  

---
